Climate Tech Resources

A curated compilation of the most important research, data, tools, and communities for building climate tech.

Essential Research Papers

Tackling Climate Change with Machine Learning Foundation

Rolnick, D. et al., 2019/2022. ACM Computing Surveys.

The seminal paper that catalyzed the modern Climate AI movement, mapping out 100+ ways machine learning can address climate mitigation and adaptation.

Read Paper

GraphCast: Learning skillful medium-range global weather forecasting AI Modeling

Lam, R. et al., 2023. Science.

Google DeepMind's breakthrough Graph Neural Network model that outperformed traditional numerical weather prediction (HRES) in accuracy and speed.

Read Paper

Toward Improved Predictions in Ungauged Basins Water/Adaptation

Kratzert, F. et al., 2019. Water Resources Research.

Landmark paper showing how LSTMs can outperform traditional physics-based hydrological models for predicting river flow, even in unmapped regions.

Read Paper

Trajectories of the Earth System in the Anthropocene Climate Science

Steffen, W. et al., 2018. PNAS.

Introduces the concept of "Hothouse Earth" and the danger of cascading tipping points in the climate system.

Recommended Books

Speed & Scale

John Doerr - An action plan for solving the climate crisis, breaking down exactly how many gigatons of emissions need to be eliminated across specific sectors (power, transportation, industry, agriculture, buildings).

Under a White Sky

Elizabeth Kolbert - An exploration of human interventions in nature, including geoengineering, and the ethical/scale challenges of trying to engineer our way out of planetary problems.

The Smart Enough City

Ben Green - A crucial critique of "smart city" techno-solutionism, arguing against deploying technology for its own sake and advocating for tech that serves civic equity.

Datasets

Google Earth Engine Data Catalog Geospatial

A multi-petabyte catalog of satellite imagery and geospatial datasets with planetary-scale analysis capabilities. Essential for any spatial climate AI work.

ERA5 Reanalysis Climate/Weather

ECMWF's comprehensive historical climate and weather data (1940-present). The gold standard training dataset for AI weather models like GraphCast and Pangu-Weather.

Climate TRACE Emissions

Facility-level global greenhouse gas emissions data estimated via satellite imagery and machine learning.

Tools & Frameworks

xarray Python Library

N-dimensional arrays in Python. The fundamental library for working with multi-dimensional climate data (NetCDF/Zarr files).

EnergyPlus Simulation

DOE's flagship whole-building energy simulation program. Used as the environment for many Reinforcement Learning HVAC optimization projects.

QGIS Desktop App

Open-source Geographic Information System. The standard local tool for visualizing and analyzing spatial data before writing code.

Communities & Organizations

Work on Climate Slack Community

The largest community dedicated to helping people pivot their careers into climate tech. Excellent jobs board and expert office hours.

Climate Change AI (CCAI) Organization

An organization of researchers and engineers facilitating the application of ML to climate change. Hosts major workshops at NeurIPS and ICLR.
